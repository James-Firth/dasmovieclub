About DAS Movie Club

We vote about once a week, one week on the theme, the following week on a movie that fits the theme

Goals of DAS Movie Club:

  • Educate ourselves and others about film
  • See movies we've been meaning to see
  • See movies we wouldn't have seen otherwise
  • Have some snacks and drinks
  • Have fun watching movies
